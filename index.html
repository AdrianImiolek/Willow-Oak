about us

Atika Mashal

Founded by Atika Mashal, a designer known for her poetic, grounded approach, Willow & Oak has grown into a collaborative studio where nature meets nuance. Atika’s work is rooted in organic forms, soft minimalism, and timeless palettes that invite stillness and soul.

Carlos Morandi

Joining her is Carlos Morandi, a detail-driven designer with a love for architectural balance and refined materials. Carlos brings structure and precision, ensuring that every space is as functional as it is graceful.

Sarah Connors

Sarah Connors completes the trio with a keen eye for visual storytelling. Her passion for textures, color harmony, and personal touches makes each project deeply intimate and quietly bold.

how we work

  1. Discovery & Connection

    We begin with a warm conversation to understand your needs, lifestyle, and the feeling you want your space to evoke. This is where we listen, observe, and align with your vision.

  2. Concept & Moodboarding

    Next, we translate your story into a curated concept — a visual direction that blends color, texture, light, and form. You’ll receive a moodboard and design proposal that captures the heart of your project.

  3. Design Development

    With a clear concept in place, we refine layout, materials, and finishes. Every detail — from fixtures to fabrics — is thoughtfully chosen to support both beauty and function.

  4. Sourcing & Coordination

    We handle all the behind-the-scenes: sourcing, ordering, and coordinating with contractors, artisans, and suppliers. Our goal is to make the process seamless and enjoyable for you.

  5. Styling & Reveal

    Once everything is in place, we style your space with intention — adding the finishing touches that make it truly yours. Then comes our favorite moment: seeing you step into your new space for the first time.
